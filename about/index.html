About

Hello my name is Jae. I’m a Computer Science student and an aspiring developer. I’ve taken courses and dabbled in various languages and frameworks over the last few years but never stayed committed to becoming an experienced developer. I’ve decided to recommit myself and my goal is to become a full stack .NET developer.

I will use this website to show my work as well as document and share everything I learn in the form of blog posts.

I have experience with HTML, CSS, Java, and C#.NET. I’m currently doing the #100DaysOfCode challenge to develop a habit of coding everyday and learning something new. Right now I plan on studying C# more and mastering the basics of programming. Follow me on Twitter or check my log on GitHub where I’ll be sharing my struggles and progressions.
