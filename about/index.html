About

I’m Jae. I’m a computer science student, programmer, and all around IT specialist.

Most of my programming and development experience is with C# and the .NET Framework. C# is my favorite programming language right now. I’ve also played with all kinds of other languages such as C, C++, Java, VB.NET, Python, JavaScript, PHP, Scheme, Clojure, and COBOL.

I’ve done some things on the front end as well with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I can build custom static websites or quickly generate them with a static site generator like Jekyll or Hugo.

I have professional experience working in IT support and HR information systems. While earning my computer science degree and advancing my programming skills, I’m currently working as a freelance IT specialist supporting small businesses in my local community with resolutions to any of their IT related issues.

About this site

This site is where I blog everything that I learn related to computer science, programming, and technology so I can document things for myself and share with others.

I also use this site to share and post about my personal projects as well as any others that I’m working on or contributing to.
